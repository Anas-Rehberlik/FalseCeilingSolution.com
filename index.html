Our Services


At False Ceiling Solutions, we offer a wide range of false ceiling services to meet the needs of our clients.
Our services include:

Gypsum board ceiling

Elevate your ceilings with stunning gypsum board designs!

Gypsum board ceilings are a popular choice for their durability and flexibility in design. Our team of experts can create custom gypsum board designs that will transform your space.

Wall punning

Transform your walls with smooth, flawless finishes!

Wall punning is a technique that involves applying a layer of plaster to walls to create a smooth, even finish. This technique is great for hiding imperfections and creating a flawless look for your walls.

3D designer ceiling

Add depth and dimension to your ceilings with our 3D designer options!

3D designer ceilings are a modern and unique way to add depth and texture to your ceilings. Our team can create custom 3D designs that will add a wow factor to any room.

Dome and curve ceiling

Make a statement with our striking dome and curve ceilings!

Dome and curve ceilings are an elegant and eye-catching option that can add a touch of sophistication to any space. Our team can create stunning designs that will elevate the look of your ceilings.

Ceiling groove and skirting groove

Add a touch of elegance to your ceilings with our groove options!

Ceiling and skirting grooves are a simple yet elegant way to add detail to your ceilings. Our team can create custom groove designs that will add a touch of sophistication to your space.

M.R. Ultra Board Ceiling

Enjoy high-quality and durable ceilings with our M.R. Ultra Board option!

M.R. Ultra Board ceilings are a great option for their strength and durability. Our team can install M.R. Ultra Board ceilings that will last for years to come.

Designer Cornis

Add a touch of elegance to your ceilings with our designer cornice options!

Designer cornices are a beautiful way to add a finishing touch to your ceilings. Our team can create custom designs that will add a touch of elegance to any space.

Profile Light Cutting

Add a modern touch to your space with our profile light cutting options!

Profile light cutting is a modern way to add lighting to your space. Our team can create custom profile designs that will add a unique and stylish touch to any room.
